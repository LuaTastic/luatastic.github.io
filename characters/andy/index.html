Andy
Andy is one of the most notable characters in TPCL. He wears a red shirt, green trousers and has a red ROBLOX visor on his head.
Trivia
• Andy works as a cashier and manager of the False Dreams Store.
• Andy was used to sell GPS devices, but they were later put off-sale for an unknown reason. However, the GPS devices can still be found in the dustbin behind the store, wooden shack or near the bunker.
• Andy's look is based off of the GASA4 cashier.
• Andy sleeps in the store. The reason for this is unknown.
• In the older (early 2021) versions of Test Place, andy was replaced by a scientist.
• Andy was replaced by a janitor in all versions from mid 2021 to 2022.
• Andy is not aware of the simulation.
