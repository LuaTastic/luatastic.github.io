Mr. Blue
Mr. Blue is one of the most notable characters in the Test Place: Remastered universe. He wears a blue shirt, black trousers, light-blue scarf and has a blue fedora. He works as a cashier in the "Dream" Store. He runs all of the Dream Stores across T.P.R.
Trivia
• Mr. Blue is pretty intelligent and has good memory, because he can count money without a cash register and memorise his products' prices.
• "Mr. Blue" is only a pseudonym. He has a real name, starting on S. He prefers to keep his name secret, and there is only 1 person that knows his actual name.
• Mr. Blue can speak and write in fluent Russian, though he never does in T.P.R.
• Mr. Blue has a friendly personality. He loves making new friends.
• He sleeps in the store at night, instead of going home, but the reason for this is unknown.
• Mr. Blue has an odd memory about some purple-shirt individual, which was, supposedly, his friend once.
