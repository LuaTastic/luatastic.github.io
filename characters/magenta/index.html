Magenta
Not to be mistaken with Lawrence.

Magenta is one of the most notable characters from Test Place: Remastered. Magenta has pinkish-purple hair, purple and black striped sweater, black trousers and yellow skin.
Trivia
• Magenta goes by the pronouns he/him.
• Magenta runs a car dealership.
• Magenta is the "employee of the year" in his car dealership company.
