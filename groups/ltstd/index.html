LuaTastic Studios

"We're a small developer group that makes... Small games."

LuaTastic Studios is a small developer group created by LuaTastic on the 17th of September, 2023. In the future this group will release some remasters and new projects.

Facts

• This group is the second ever group created by LuaTastic, while Test Place Official is the first.

• This group is officially related to this website.

• There are 2 people running this group: Michal57012 as the head developer and LuaTastic as the founder/owner.
