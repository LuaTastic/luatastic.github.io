Group Warning

CZ developers minigames is a small developer group, with the purpose of making "mini" games. It is currently unknown what games they are working on. This group has been created somewhere around August 30th - September 1st 2023.

Facts

• The administrator and head developer of this group, Michal57012, is the former builder and CO-CEO of Test Place: Classics and Test Place: Remastered.

• The group's icon is the same as the original cz developers group's, but with the logo being pink instead of blue, and "mini" written below CZ.

• This group is a sub-group of cz developers.
