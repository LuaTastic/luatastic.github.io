Test Place Official Group

Test Place Official Group is a group made by LuaTastic (formerly known as DaneliitTwo/ScriptException) for the Test Place Series. Currently this group has over 60 members.

This group gives you perks in all of the test place series games, including: Test Place Secret Research Facility, Test Place: Classics and Test Place: Remastered.

Perks
Test Place Secret Research Facility

1. As a security guard of ANY rank you get the access to the security room.
The weapons you get depends on your Security rank. If you are Tier 2 Security+, you get access to the taser. If you are Elite Security+, you get access to the shotgun and the taser.

2. As the CEO/dev you get access to all doors, weapons and you gain access to an exclusive AK-47 placed on a wall within the spawn area.

Test Place: Classics

1. If your rank is Science Team or higher, you will get a clipboard.

2. If your rank is Head of Security or higher, you get a riot shield and a shotgun.

3. If your rank is warden or higher, you get access to the control room in the facility.

4. If your rank is Developer or higher, you get access to the nuclear warhead controls. However, you have to know a 16-digit code to enter the nuclear warhead sector. If you type in the wrong code, you will get shot by a rocket launcher.

Test Place: Remastered (abandoned project)

1. If your rank is trainee security or higher, you get access to certain security doors placed around the facility, for example the control room on the entrance, security rooms on the lower levels of the facility and the security breakroom. You also get access to LOG consoles and the lounge.

2. If your rank is Elite security or higher, you get access to the MP-90 and the Shotgun located in the breakroom.
